FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions
User Capabilities
  • How do I create a game?
    You can only create a game if you are a logged in as an organizer. You will have to send a request to malicsia7l@gmail.com containing your name, details, contact number, and reason for registering as such. An administrator will keep in touch with you.
  • Who can add, update and delete sports?
    Only organizers can add, update and delete sports.
  • Who can pick and add the sports to be included in a game?
    Only the organizers who created the game can add the sports to be included in it.
  • Who can delete a game?
    Only organizers can delete a game.
  • Who can add users/teams to a particular sport or game?
    Only organizers can add users/teams to a particular sport or game. Once a team manager/coach creates a team for a game, a request is automatically sent to the organizer of the game.
  • Do I need to login to view the games and results?
    No. You can browse the public profiles of Games, Sports, Competitors, Organizers, and Organizations even without logging into the system.
  • How do I become a coach?
    A competitor can become a coach, they will be able to create a team but are not able to create a game.
  • What is the difference between an organizer and an organization?
    An organizer is an institution or an entity that manages a game. An organization is the unifying entity for teams. Each Team belongs to a specific organization.
  • Who can view and manage my profile?
    Only the owner can manage their profile, but other users can view their profiles.
  • Who can deactivate and activate accounts?
